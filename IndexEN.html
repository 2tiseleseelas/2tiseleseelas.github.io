Eles&Elas

Class Project

The topic addressed in our class project is Gender Equality, also known as sexual equality or equality of the sexes, is the state of having equal access to resources and opportunities regardless of gender, including economic participation and decision-making. And the state of valuing different behaviors, aspirations and needs.

Our objective is to promote the same and for this to be achieved, 2ºTIS made a game called Eles&Elas that will be available for download below.

App Eles&Elas

The game eles/elas was built with several platforms and several students from the 2nd TIS class.
The same was done because of the “Gender Equality” project.
The game consists of selecting between two people (cards) (whose they are female and male) for the same profession, whether mechanic, computer technician, architect, among others...
