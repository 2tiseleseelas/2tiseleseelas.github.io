Korfball Tournament

Korfball Tournament - 2nd TIS

On June 27, the Korfeball tournament will be held, which is being organized by the 2nd year class of the Computer Technician - Systems course.

This tournament will be held within the scope of the class project on gender equality, being a Korfball tournament as this was the first modality to be practiced by mixed teams, formed by men and women in equal numbers.

Talk to your colleagues, whether they are in your class or not, form a team and come have fun with us on June 27th!
